# 🌿 THE RECLAIMED — Deployment Guide

## You need 3 files total:
- server.js
- index.html
- package.json

---

## 🚀 DEPLOY ON RAILWAY (Free, No Credit Card)

### Step 1 — Create a GitHub account (if you don't have one)
→ https://github.com → Sign up free

### Step 2 — Create a new GitHub repository
1. Go to https://github.com/new
2. Name it: `the-reclaimed`
3. Set it to Public
4. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3 — Upload your files
1. Click **Add file** → **Upload files**
2. Drag and drop all 3 files: `server.js`, `index.html`, `package.json`
3. Click **Commit changes**

### Step 4 — Deploy on Railway
1. Go to https://railway.app
2. Click **Start a New Project**
3. Click **Deploy from GitHub repo**
4. Connect your GitHub account
5. Select your `the-reclaimed` repository
6. Railway will auto-detect it as Node.js and deploy!

### Step 5 — Get your link
1. In Railway, click your project
2. Click **Settings** → **Networking** → **Generate Domain**
3. You'll get a link like: `the-reclaimed.up.railway.app`
4. Share this link with friends! ✅

## 🎮 HOW TO PLAY

### Controls
- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** — Move
- **Click** — Interact (harvest trees, attack animals, pick up items)
- **C** — Toggle Crafting Menu
- **1–7** — Hotbar quick-select
- **Right-click inventory slot** — Use item (eat food, drink water, use bandage)
- **Click weapon in inventory** — Equip/unequip
- **Enter** — Focus chat

### Survival Tips
- Your **hunger and thirst drain constantly** — find food and water early
- **Chop trees** first to get wood (equip wooden axe → click trees)
- **Craft weapons** to fight animals and other players
- **Magical spirit foxes** drop rare crystals — they're hard to find!
- **Die = start over** with a new identity (permadeath!)

### Crafting Recipes
- 🪓 Wooden Axe: 5 Wood
- 🗡️ Spear: 6 Wood + 1 Rope
- 🏹 Bow: 4 Wood + 2 Rope
- 🩹 Bandage: 2 Old Cloth
- ➶ Arrow: 2 Wood + 1 Iron Scrap
- 🔪 Iron Knife: 4 Iron Scrap + 2 Wood
- 🔥 Campfire: 8 Wood + 2 Stone
- 🏕️ Shelter: 20 Wood + 4 Rope

## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

**Game won't load:** Make sure all 3 files are uploaded to GitHub
**Can't connect:** Wait 2 minutes after deploying for Railway to boot
**Friends can't join:** Share the full Railway URL including https://
